Why should you consider growing cactuse?

Cactuses are incredible household companions to people that don't have a lot of time to care for plants. This aspect comes from the fact that cactuses don't need no fancy soil. They thrive in poor soil. Also them being suculents means you shouldn't water them very often. Only thing that that you need to remember is to water them at all. For lazy person such as me, even this can prove to be a significant task.
Main reason to even keep such a plant is that when it has flowers, more often than not thay are quite spectacular. Also each cactus grows very different from the previous one, making interesting shapes in the process.
